Detection of Floating Plastics (Experimental)

Identification of plastic and marine litter is a new frontier in the application of sattelite imagery. Here we attempt to identify potential targets on the ocean surface across the Southern half of North Male' atoll and South Male' atoll, with Sentinel-2 high resolution imagery using the Plastic Index (Themistocleous. et.al, 2020) and the Floating Debri Index (Biermann. et.al, 2020). . Initial results presnt here shows that it is viable even in an atoll contest, identfying targets such as speed boats and potentially floating pastic as well. However further refinement and study is needed to make a complete assesment.

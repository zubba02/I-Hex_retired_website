Atoll Future Scenarios and Experimental Features

Currently the following future scenarios and experimental features are offered to be explored :

  1. ★ Detection of Plastic targets ★
  2. Atoll Future Maximum Near Surface Air Temperature Explorer
    (Atoll Monthly Surface Sea Water Temperature explorer for future projections using 2 different Representative Concentration Pathways (RCP) scenarios. The RCP 8.5 scenario corresponds to a business as usual situation. The RCP 4.5 scenario corresponds to an intermediate situation where emissions peak around 2040 and then decline.)
  3. Atoll Future Precipitation Explorer
    (Atoll Monthly Surface Sea Water Temperature explorer for future projections using 2 different Representative Concentration Pathways (RCP) scenarios.)
  4. ★ Identification of ship tracks 2012 - 2016 using AIS data ★
